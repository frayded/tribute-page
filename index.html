H. Agus Salim

The Grand Old Man. He, who was leading by suffering.

Haji Agus Salim was a journalist, diplomat, and state official from Indonesia. He was born on October 8, 1884, and died on November 4, 1954. Between 1947 and 1949, he was Indonesia's Minister of Foreign Affairs.
